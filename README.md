The game "Spike Balls" is a 3D multiplayer, player-versus-player (PvP) game where two players compete to stay in the arena. The player who falls off the arena loses the round, and the other player earns a point. The game continues until both players agree to stop. The game's name comes from the characters, which are two spiked balls, adding a unique element to the mechanics. The ball's spikes play a crucial role, allowing players to potentially grab and hold onto the spikes, influencing gameplay and making it more challenging.
Developed in Unity and programmed with C#, the game was created to provide a dynamic gaming experience.
